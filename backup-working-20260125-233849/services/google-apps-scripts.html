Google Apps Scripts

Automate Google Workspace workflows, integrate systems, and eliminate repetitive tasks. Smart solutions for busy business people.

Google Workspace is powerful, but it can be so much more. We build custom Google Apps Scripts that automate repetitive tasks, integrate your systems, and eliminate the manual work that eats up your day. Smart automation, fast implementation, immediate time savings.

What We Automate

  • Google Sheets Automation: Auto-populate data, generate reports, send notifications, format cells, and process large datasets automatically
  • Gmail Workflows: Auto-respond to emails, categorize messages, create tasks from emails, and automate email-based processes
  • Google Drive Management: Organize files automatically, move documents based on rules, generate folder structures, and manage permissions
  • Calendar Automation: Schedule meetings automatically, send reminders, sync events across calendars, and manage availability
  • Form Processing: Automatically process Google Form submissions, send confirmations, update spreadsheets, and trigger workflows
  • Data Integration: Connect Google Workspace with external systems via APIs, sync data between platforms, and automate data transfers
  • Document Generation: Auto-create documents, reports, and presentations from templates using data from your spreadsheets

Common Use Cases

Automated Reporting: Generate daily, weekly, or monthly reports automatically from your data, formatted and ready to share.

Data Entry Elimination: Automatically transfer data between systems, eliminating manual data entry and reducing errors.

Workflow Automation: Create approval processes, task assignments, and notifications that run automatically based on triggers.

Email Management: Automatically categorize, respond to, or forward emails based on rules, saving hours of inbox management.

Invoice & Document Generation: Create invoices, contracts, or other documents automatically from spreadsheet data.

Why Google Apps Scripts?

No Additional Software: Works directly within Google Workspace - no new tools to learn, no additional subscriptions to pay for.

Seamless Integration: Scripts integrate perfectly with Google Sheets, Gmail, Drive, Calendar, and other Google services you already use.

Cost Effective: Google Apps Scripts is free to use. You only pay for the development time, not ongoing licensing fees.

Fast Development: We can build and deploy scripts quickly, often in days rather than weeks.

Easy Maintenance: Scripts are stored in the cloud, easy to update, and accessible from anywhere.

Our Development Process

Identify Opportunities: We analyze your workflows to find repetitive tasks that can be automated. Often, we find opportunities you didn't even know existed.

Design the Solution: We map out the automation, identify triggers, and design the workflow before writing any code.

Build & Test: We develop the script, test it thoroughly, and ensure it handles edge cases and errors gracefully.

Deploy & Train: We set up the script in your Google Workspace, provide training on how it works, and document everything for future reference.

Ongoing Support: We're available to modify scripts, add features, or fix issues as your needs evolve.
